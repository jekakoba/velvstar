About Us Our Services

OUR MISSION

Customer Retention:
Fashion Edition.

Founded in 2019 (then as “DG Marketing”) as an all-in-one agency in Sweden, we made a strategic decision in early 2022 to focus solely on Email and SMS marketing for fashion brands in the eCommerce space.

Since then, our services have consistently generated from 25% all the way up to 40% of our client's revenue. As your dedicated marketing partner, we are fluent in the language of your industry and committed to championing your brand's success. 

Trust us to tailor your brand for your people, and create customers that keep coming back.
